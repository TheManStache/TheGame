How to Play

Welcome to the world of Pokémon! In this game, you will be tasked with choosing a team of Pokémon and battling against other trainers. Each Pokémon has basic attacks and one unique ability. You can use these attacks to defeat your opponents and become the ultimate Pokémon trainer.

To start the game, first choose your team of Pokémon. You can choose from five different tiers of Pokémon, each stronger than the last. Once you've chosen your team, you'll enter into the buying phase. Here, you can purchase items to help your Pokémon in battle.

After the buying phase, you'll enter into the battle phase. Your Pokémon will automatically attack any enemy Pokémon within its range, and will automatically use its ability based on certain criteria. The battle will continue until one team has been defeated.
